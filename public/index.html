Budget Planner App

This budget planner application uses React. I created it when my wife and I bought our first home. We were recent graduates, and needed an effective way to track our new monthly expenses and budget. It has a simple interface where the user can select income or expense and enter the amount.

I also incorporated local storage so when the user leaves the application, the information they entered stores automatically for their return.

VIN Decoder App

This web App uses the CarMD API to return back a vehicle's year, make and model, trim and engine type based off of the car VIN entered by the user. Though simple, this application is extremely useful for users looking to confirm details about a vehicle they are purchasing, selling or in need of parts for.
